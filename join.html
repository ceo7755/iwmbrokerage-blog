What's the difference between an "A" lead and a "B" lead? ▼

“A” leads are leads that have never been sold. They are exclusive to the agent for five weeks and their price depends upon contract level. For new agents, they start at about $35.

“B” Leads or Bonus Leads include B1, B2, B3, B4, & B5 leads. The difference is based on how old the lead is.

  • B1 Lead: Distributed once as an A lead but still has not been sold. Now $7.99 each. Starts at five weeks of age and can go up to eight months.
  • B2 Lead: Distributed twice. Once as an A lead then as a B1 but still has not been sold. Now $6.99 each. Starts at 10 weeks of age and can go up to 10 months.
  • B3 Lead: Distributed up to three times. Once as an A lead then as a B1 & B2 but still hasn’t been sold. Now $5.99 each. Starts at 15 weeks of age and can go up to 12 Months.
  • B4 Lead: Distributed up to four times as an A lead, B1, B2, and B3 Lead but still has not been sold. Now $3.99 each. Starts at 20 weeks and can go to 18+ months.
  • B5 Lead: Distributed up to five times. These leads are $1.99 and are between five months and 35 months old. These are exclusive to the agent as a B5 lead for three months.
