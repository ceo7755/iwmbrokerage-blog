Infinite Banking

A specially designed policy to build cash value, allowing you to "become your own banker" by borrowing against that cash value for purchases, investments, or needs instead of relying on traditional banks or lenders.

Learn More

You repay those policy loans to yourself (with interest going back into your policy), so your money continues to grow tax-advantaged through guaranteed interest and potential dividends, creating a personal banking system that recaptures interest typically paid to financial institutions.
