At IWM, we believe that financial security and wealth creation go hand in hand with education. Founded in 2019, our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to not just grow, but protect their wealth through educated decisions in insurance, retirement planning, and beyond. We have protected families with over $10 million in benefit amount, written over $1,000,000 in annual premium volume, & helped over 500 clients with their insurance & retirement needs.

Our Mission

To solve the pandemic of financial illiteracy. We aim to provide peace of mind by offering tailored insurance and retirement strategies that protect you and your loved ones. With a focus on plain language education and the state's lowest premiums, we empower our clients to make informed decisions about their financial future.

Why Choose IWM?

  • Expertise: Over a decade of experience in insurance and wealth management.
  • Education-First Approach: We provide insights and resources to help you make confident decisions.
  • Personalized Solutions: Tailored plans that fit your unique needs and goals. We are advisors, not salespeople.
  • Brokers, not Agents: We do not have a tie to a singular carrier. We partner with over 40+ carriers in your state, so we have them compete against each other, which results in the most comprehensive coverage at your state's lowest premiums.
  • Innovative Thinking: We stay ahead of trends, including the integration of digital currencies into financial planning.
